About Me

Campbell Ramage is a Photographer and Picture Editor with over 20 years experience working in the newspaper industry. Campbell studied Photography and AV technology at Glasgow's College of Building and Printing, before taking up a role on the picture desk at The Scottish Daily Mail, where he has covered breaking news stories for over two decades. Away from the hustle and bustle of the newsroom Campbell uses his keen eye for a picture to photograph life in and around his home city of Glasgow. Working to a theme of 'positivity', in 2022 he received the acclaim of the Scottish Parliament for his portrait exhibition 'Maryhill Is Wonderful' a photographic study of the people who make a positive contribution to life in one of the cities most deprived areas. Campbell recently joined forces with poet Julie McNeill for their book 'We Are Scottish Football' which was shorltisted for the Illustrated Sports Book of the Year. His love of the Beautiful Game is a common theme in his work, and he has worked closely with groups across Glasgow who aim to use football as a force for good.
